Welcome to my page about Autism Awareness

This page is to raise awareness about the Infinity Autism Symbol!

A more preferred symbol!

The Rainbow Infinity Sign is the more preferred symbol among the autistic community. It represents the infinite possibilites of those on the spectrum. The possibilites being skills, interest, abilities and how unique an individual on the spectrum can be. Since Autism isn't a linear disorder and more of a spectrum, it makes sense that that infinty symbol would be a more appropriate symbol to represent that community in a more prideful way by celebrating the differences and possibilites of those with Autism.
If you are looking to support Autism please consider products that use a more supportive symbol that celebrates the differences in people with Autism and the infinite possibilities!

No More Puzzle Piece!

The puzzle piece was first used in 1963. It was designed by Gerald Gasson, who was a Board member of the Autistic Children Association(NAC), and also a parent of individuals with Autism. This made it easy for the symbol to be accepted. The original symbol profiled a child crying in the center of a puzzle piece. This was meant to represent the suffering of individuals with Autism over their differences. The Autistic comunity celebrates their differences rather then consider themselves suffering just because they are neurodivergent. The community wants to move away from this symbol since they are not a puzzle, a riddle, and they are not missing any peices. The origin of the symbol and metaphors that have come along with it could be harmful to indviduals with Autsim.
